Sea Lamprey water depth on spawning migration in the White River, Michigan 2021.

We had 57 fish tracks through the White River. A subset of 19 fish were tagged with pressure tags to determine fish depth. Sea lamprey were a maximum 3.5667046 meters above the river bottom.
